Music Classes

Music classes are offered as electives in both juinor and senior years. These classes will cover basic music theory, sight reading and learning how to play different instrments. Students have many oppotunities through out the school year to participate in workshops and excursions to improve their skillsets.

Seniors will have the oppotunity to partake in the Sydney Performing Arts Trip where they will attend workshops to sharpen their skills and experiance live theatre performances.

Students may also be asked to perform in extra curricular bands or groups or perform at mass if they perform exceptionally well. Please note that participation in these events is not compolsory but it is encouraged.

Drama Classes

Drama classes are offered as electives in both juinor and senior years. These classes will cover basic drama theory, script writing and acting.

Students have many oppotunities through out the school year to participate in workshops and excursions to improve their skillsets. Senior students will also have the oppotunity to participate in the Senior Arts Sydney trip where they will be allowed to partake in some exclusive workshops to improve on their dance abilities.

Students may also be asked to perform in extra curricular groups like Spotlight, the afterschool theatre program, or perform at masses and assemblies. Please note that participation in these events is not compolsory but it is encouraged.

Wollongong


Wollongong, affectionately called "The Gong" by locals, is a place full of entertainment. Wollongong is hoem to Science space and the Australian Motorlife museum. Concerts are no stranger to Wollongong. Many well known Australian artists (such as Jimmy Barnes and Daryl Braithwaite) make sure to set up stage whilst on their tours.

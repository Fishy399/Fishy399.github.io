Music Lessons

At JPC we offer music lessons for most instruments, including Guitar, drums, piano, brass instruments, woodwind instruments and many more. We are also looking at expanding this range of instruments to include stringed instruments like violin and cello in upcoming years.

Music lessons take place during school hours and may take time out of class or lunch time. Costs vary depending on what instrment is selected, please check out the expression of interest form below to get a better idea on the pricing of each instrument. We also offer instrment hire for students who do not own the instrument that they want to learn. The hire cost is $52 per term.

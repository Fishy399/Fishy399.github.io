Berarra


Berrara is a quiet town just outside sussex inlet. It has accomidation for all vacation styles, from camping to staying in a cabin. Berarra is also a great place to get away from busy city life or a change of pace. Berarra beach connects to a lagoon at high tide. This lagoon is home to small fish and is a warmer temerature than the ocean. The beach is also dog friendly, so feel free to bring your canine pal along!

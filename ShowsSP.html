Shows

At JPC we offer the oppotunity for students to participat in stage productions. Our first stage production was "The Greatest Show" and it allowed students to really get involved in the stages of production in regards to script writing, costumes, choreography and music. We plan to do another production next year so keep your ears open for any annoucments, and don't forget to check out the "What's On" page to see other Performing Arts events happening in and around JPC.

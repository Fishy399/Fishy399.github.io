Dance Classes


Dance classes are offered as electives in both juinor and senior years. These classes will cover basic dance theory and choreography in a range of diffenet styles including Hip Hop, Contemporary, Tap, Jazz, Ballet and many more!

Students have many oppotunities through out the school year to participate in workshops and excursions to improve their skillsets. Senior students will also have the oppotunity to participate in the Senior Arts Sydney trip where they will be allowed to partake in some exclusive workshops to improve on their dance abilities.

Students may also be asked to perform in extra curricular groups like Dancefest or perform at masses and assemblies if they perform exceptionally well. Please note that participation in these events is not compolsory but it is encouraged.

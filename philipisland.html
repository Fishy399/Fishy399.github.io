Philip Island


Philip Island is a day trip from melbourne by boat. The main tourist attractions are the wildlife and the Philip Island race track. Philip Island is home to a large colony of Australian fur seals and a colony of penguins. Off the coast of the island, whales can be spotted during the migrating months.
